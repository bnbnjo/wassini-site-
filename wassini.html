FAQ

Is this a courier company?
No. Wassini is a marketplace that connects buyers and travelers already headed the same way.
How do you keep it safe?
Verified profiles, optional ID check, ratings, in-app chat, escrow, and 2FA. Keep communication in-app.
What items are allowed?
Light personal goods that comply with airline, customs, and local law. No cash, contraband, hazardous or restricted items.
When do travelers get paid?
After the buyer confirms delivery, the escrow releases the payout minus the platform fee.
Do you support Arabic?
Yes — the app and website support Arabic and English.
